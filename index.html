Our Story

Founded by Suraj Lama in 2015, our restaurant brings the authentic taste of Himalayan momos to your neighborhood. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the traditional flavors while adding our own modern twist.

At Suraj Lama Momos, we believe in using only the freshest ingredients to create dumplings that not only taste amazing but also nourish your body. Our chefs are trained in the art of Momos-making, ensuring that every plate that leaves our kitchen is perfect.

Whether you're a long-time fan of momos or trying them for the first time, we promise an unforgettable dining experience that will keep you coming back for more.
