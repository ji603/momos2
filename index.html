Our Story

Founded in 2010, Deccan Dosa brings the authentic flavors of South Indian cuisine to your neighborhood. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the traditional taste while adding our own modern twist.

At Deccan Dosa, we believe in using only the freshest ingredients to create dishes that not only taste amazing but also nourish your body. Our chefs are trained in the art of South Indian cooking, ensuring that every dish that leaves our kitchen is perfect.

Whether you're a long-time fan of South Indian cuisine or trying it for the first time, we promise an unforgettable dining experience that will keep you coming back for more.
